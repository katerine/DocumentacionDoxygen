Descripción detallada

The crossover operator randomly selects two Chromosomes from the population and "mates" them by randomly picking a gene and then swapping that gene and all subsequent genes between the two Chromosomes. The two modified Chromosomes are then added to the list of candidate Chromosomes.

If you work with CompositeGenes, this operator expects them to contain genes of the same type (e.g. IntegerGene). If you have mixed types, please provide your own crossover operator.

This CrossoverOperator supports both fixed and dynamic crossover rates. A fixed rate is one specified at construction time by the user. This operation is performed 1/m_crossoverRate as many times as there are Chromosomes in the population. Another possibility is giving the crossover rate as a percentage. A dynamic rate is determined by this class on the fly if no fixed rate is provided.
